26-week UA audit across six mobile advertising platforms using synthetic campaign data.

Data: Realistic impression ranges, CTR distributions, install rates, spend budgets, and ARPU per channel. Noise and outlier weeks injected to mimic real variance.

Outlier removal: Weeks outside 1.5x IQR per channel on CPI, CTR, IR, or ROAS were excluded.

Scoring: Min-max normalisation on four dimensions combined as: CPI 35%, ROAS 30%, IR 20%, CTR 15%. Scaled 0-100.

Recommendations: Scale / Increase / Maintain / Review assigned per rank and ROAS thresholds.
